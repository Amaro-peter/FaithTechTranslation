# Resources for Create Leaders

As a Create leader guiding your community or project, you play a crucial
role in ensuring its success and alignment with redemptive technology
principles. This guide provides a comprehensive set of resources to
support you in this important work.

## Support

As you walk through these training resources, you may find yourself with more
questions. If you need further assistance to get prepared for your Create cycle,
reach out to us on [Slack | #create-general](https://faithtechhub.slack.com/).

## Reflection Questions

- How can these resources help you better integrate faith and technology in your 4D cycle project?
- Which areas do you feel you need the most support in as a Create leader?
- How can you use these resources to empower and equip other members of your team?

Remember, these resources are meant to support and guide you, but the most
valuable resource is the leading of the Holy Spirit throughout your 4D cycle
journey. Regularly seek God's wisdom as you use these tools to lead your
community in redemptive technology development.
